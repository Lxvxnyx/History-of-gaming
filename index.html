Introduction

Video games started in the 1970s and have changed a lot over time. In the early days, games like Pong and Space Invaders were very popular. Later came consoles like the Nintendo and PlayStation. Now, games are online and very realistic.

FAQ

When did video games start?

They started in the 1970s with arcade games.

What was the first console?

The first console was the Magnavox Odyssey in 1972.

Are games only for kids?

No, people of all ages play games today.
